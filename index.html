üí° How Community Governance Works

Problem: Customer orders $40 pizza, promises to be home, but isn't there when driver arrives. Driver waits 20 minutes.
Solution: Pizza place reports with evidence (delivery timestamp, call logs). Community votes "unacceptable." Customer loses 25 MS and gets warning.

Result: Others are honest about item conditions.
